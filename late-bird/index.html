Late Bird

Are you a sunrise person or a sunset?

Most people are one or the other.

I suppose you could be both, you overachieving son of a...

But choose.

Sun up?

Or sun down?

Dawn?

Or dusk?

I've always been a night owl.

Not an early bird.

And golden hours are sacred in this house.

When day and night meet.

They nod at each other as they pass.

One clocking out, the other clocking in.

Golden hour leads to sunset.

So it's a win-win for me.

Better than a double rainbow.
