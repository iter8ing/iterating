Can't Make You Drink

This is a happy place.

My happy place.

I got a lot of them.

Home.

The open road.

But I repeat myself.

Maybe sometimes I rant too much.

When I should be happier.

I should be happier here.

A musings column, this is, not a tabloid.

I should be more amused.

More amusing.

And so I shall be.

There's a 60 mile stretch of impassible rain forest smack dab in the middle but that gets in the way of a good story.

You can drive the whole thing (almost).

Pretty amazing.

Now would you want to?

I mean, that's the whole shebang, ain't it?

Horse, this is water.

Neigh!
