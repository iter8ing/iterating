Anyone Feel A Draft?

I started writing about two things and erased them both.

One was about being vigilant outside as we approach warmer seasons.

The other was about Day 3 of the NFL Draft.

I erased them both.

I don't know why.

I will say, be careful, out there.

And I love Day 3 of the NFL Draft.

I feel like we should have corporate drafts for the Fortune 500.

Scouts in college classrooms.

An academic combine in auditoriums.

Boardrooms watching highlights of volunteer tape.

Talking heads arguing over who Nvidia will take in the first round.

Actually, that sounds terrible.

Or does it?
