Not For Everyone

If you're wondering.

You need to find your people.

Maybe more.

At its punchline.

I tend to like the latter more.

I mean, don't be fake, but I like the belly bwahahaers.

It just tickles me elbow.

So don't be throwing any courtesy giggles over here.

The people that get "it" and have "it."

That's who you want to be.

The people that "get" it.
