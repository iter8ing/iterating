Jive Turkeys

Someone once said to me: "You're so different!" Or something to that effect.

They didn't mean it as an insult, quite the opposite. But it's happened. A lot. And I would always have some derivative of the same response, "What are you? Normal?!?"

And I'd scoff and sneer at the "Normal" part. Almost like it felt disdainful to even say the word.

Normal.

Eww.

Who wants to be that?

I sure didn't.

Although I will admit, normal is getting pretty weird these days, it's still not what I want to be.

It's not who I am.

I figure the people who find this silly place will only understand me if they understand this post. If they get it.

Normal sucks.

There I said it.

Happy Thanksgiving Eve!
