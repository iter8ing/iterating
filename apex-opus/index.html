Apex Opus

Today is like a second Sunday.

It feels like that to me.

And so, I don't want to write too much, because I already did.

But here's something interesting.

In a good life, I think that having a magnum opus is essential.

Having a great work.

Having made something of tremendous value.

If only for yourself. And your loved ones.

An impact.

But even more important is having an apex opus.

Your highest work.

Your grandmasterpiece.

Your verse.

As in, what will be the headline on your obituary?

And I mean that in a good way.

In the best way.

I am iterating on this big idea.

To dreams.
