More Than One Bruce

Boy, I think I'm running out of things to say.

When I was a kid, I'd stay up at night and listen to the radio.

Now, a lot of the times it was to listen to music.

But then I found a dude who would spend all night giving advice to people.

On all sorts of things from money, business, to general life advice.

His name was Bruce Williams.

He had a deep, resonant, voice and it had a little New York City vibe, so I was hooked.

Bruce was honest, helpful, and rarely sugar coated anything.

And during a time I was searching for answers (this happens from time to time, I see), I found a mentor on the air.

All of that to say Bruce is no longer with us.

His last show was in 2013 (by then a podcast) at the young age of 81.

Passed away a few years after.

Some beautiful people have archived his stuff online.

And I just recently found him again on YouTube.

So I've been sitting here in my time machine.

Being a kid again.

Listening and learning.

Raising a glass of Coke Zero (on ice) to BW.

"Hello, Des Moines, Iowa!"
