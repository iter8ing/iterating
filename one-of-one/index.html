One of One

You know something?

You are one in a million.

Truly.

There are only about 8,300 of you walking this blue marble.

What?

Does knowing there's a small town's worth of you running around make it less special?

Fine.

You're one in a billion!

Doing math. There's still sevenish others just like me??

One of eight!

Even at one in a billy, you're still not unique.

And we're not even counting other marbles.

There's still seven other versions of you walking around picking their nose being productive and reading musings columns.

One of the biggest fears of the supremacy of artificial intellect is that we'll lose that uniqueness of being human.

But I mean, aren't we already kinda there?

Humans are sort of the same, now.

Look around.

You see it.

Similar templates.

Repeated over and over.

Like the character generator got lazy and just started mass spawning mother mothers.

Who do you know that marches to the beat of their own 808?

To one of ones.

Thank you for being you.
