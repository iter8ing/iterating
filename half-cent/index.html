Half Cent

What would your childhood version of you think of you now?

Like 11 year old you.

What would they think?

At 11, I would think I'd now be a retired hall of famer who spent his entire career with the New York Mets.

Won a few MVPs, a few triple crowns, and, most importantly, a few championship rings.

Of course, I didn't peak in my smashing bombs days until my late 30s, and sadly my field was slow-pitch church leagues by then.

I'd like to think 11 year old me would be staggered, just the same, by what's happened.

I mean, he was sorta right.

I'm sorta retired.

Sort of a hall of famer (in my own mind).

And I won one ring.

"That's not what I meant," he'd say.

And he's right.

It's so much better, kid.
