Journeys

It occurs to me that is is sort of my journal.

I would say private journal but it's on the friggin' internet.

So I won't.

It's my journal.

I've written a lot in my life. Always filtered, though.

This is as close as I have gotten to being "me" but it's still heavily colandered.

I don't want to share everything.

I think that's the art, right?

What to share, what not to.

People seem to trend two ways: They either create a highlight reel or throw a pity party.

But as a stoic of sorts, I believe in golden means.

Goldilocks means.

Not too hot, not too cold. 

Juuuuuuust right.

What is that?

70% highlights, 30% rants?

50/50?

Bah, data. Always with the data.

I'm more of a vibe guy.
