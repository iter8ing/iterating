Danger Danger

I remember a line that goes something like, you know you are old when young men no longer find you dangerous.

You know, spidey senses tingling, tapping their friends to get their attention, and getting out of the way (or getting ready to fight).

It gets me to think about being older, kinder, gentler.

I always try to think, even in the most negative of times, "What does this make possible?"

There is strength in appearing weak. 

There is power in being dismissed.

There is freedom in being unnoticed.

Too much of my youth was spent trying to be seen. In my own way. But it's true.

Wisdom teaches you not to feed the wild animals, otherwise they follow you home.

Keyboards and warriors.

Look out!
