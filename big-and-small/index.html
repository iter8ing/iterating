Big and Small

Quantum is one of my favorite words.

I got a lot of them.

Actualization.

Starfaring.

Iterating.

But quantum's up there.

You want to know why?

Because it means two very different things at the same time.

It means two opposite things simultaneously.

Quantum can be defined as something so small it's practically zero.

The notch right above nothing.

The smallest of the smallest.

At the same time, quantum can also refer to something very big.

Significant.

The giant leaps.

This is not even to mention its use in physics, mechanics, computing.

It's the word de jour, a polarizing one to be sure (my favorites usually are), but it'll likely be an important word from here out.

I'm all about important words.
