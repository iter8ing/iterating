Treasure

Are your memories treasures?

Or are your visions of the future the treasure?

What about the present?

Is right now considered a treasure?

They all seem pretty valuable.

Treasure hunters are an interesting group.

Chasing futures where they hope to find the past right now or soonish.

Someone's past, found in someone else's present, for yet another someone else's future.

Actually finding the treasure has all sorts of drama with it.

You may not even be able to keep it.

But the chase is yours.

How big is your fortune?

Is it vaulted?

Is it in the cloud?

Or can you touch it?

Are you the treasure? Yours or anyone else's?

Somewhere between remembering and imagining is present day.

Vault, earth, cloud.

I'd say carpe diem or some ess, but I ain't latin.

Sic Transit Gloria.
