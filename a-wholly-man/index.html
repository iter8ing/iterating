A Wholly Man

Who are you?

I mean that in the most literal way.

Who are you?

And how do you define yourself?

Most people would answer the question like I'm asking what their name is.

And I love names.

But what I am asking is more:

What do you represent?

How do you wish to be defined?

What should I call you?

My umma when I was still in her womb had a dream that her baby to be was all growns up and dressed in a black flowing robe. This convinced her I was destined to be a judge.

And well, I guess I am.

Got a law degree.

Got a law license.

Got black on (no robes though).

I do have a gavel or two.

I call myself the "Supreme Court" but only in my house.

Only to my kids.

But we all get a good laugh.

I think grandmasters wear those.

So sayeth, The Supreme Court!
