Good and Bad

Hi.

I don't know what to write today.

So I'll just say thanks.

Thank you.

For being good.

I don't think good people are told that enough.

Thank you for being a good human being.

Now, that presupposes some things.

So don't make an arse outta me here.

If there's some work you gotta do in this area, today's as good a day as any to start.

I would begin at the beginning, and be good to those you love the most.

And those who love you the most.

May they be the same people.

Are they the same people?

Who you love and who loves you.

And are they good?

Oh, and I just want to remind you that bad isn't the opposite of good. 

At least not the way I use it.

It's a partner of good.

A protector.

It does the dirty work.

You're thinking of the "e" word, if you're wondering about good's enemy.

Stop loving and being loved by that.

Good day.
