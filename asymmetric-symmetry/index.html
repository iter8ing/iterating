Asymmetric Symmetry

We all love symmetry.

Balance.

Perfection.

Order.

But that doesn't mean we should dislike asymmetry.

In fact, many opposites of truth are not lies, but another truth.

Symmetry is perfect.

Asymmetry is imperfectly perfect.

I love asymmetry.

It's where all the juice is.

I'd like to think life is asymmetric.

20 seconds of insane courage can change your family tree.

That's staggering asymmetry.

We all love the curves of a perfect circle.

But we should also love the jagged edges of a market.

Up down. Up down. Up (hopefully).

Let's face it, you wouldn't be in the game if it only went up.

The downs keep you in.

Puts the cork in it.

On one side, we have harmony, balance, perfection.

On the other, we have fun house mirrors.

Which way to look?

Both are equal (even in its inequality).

Both are beautiful.

Both are true.

They are opposites.

And they attract.

To monster outcomes.

May your life be balanced with imbalance.

An imperfect infinity loop.
